Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 26, 2023

Your privacy is of utmost importance to Payday. This Privacy Policy explains how Payday (the "App") collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • No Direct Personal Identifiable Information: Payday is designed to be privacy-first. We do NOT collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information that directly identifies you.
  • Financial Data: Any financial data you input or link within the app (e.g., bank account balances, transactions) is processed and stored locally on your device. We do not transmit this data to our servers or any third parties.

Non-Personal Information:

  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymized, aggregated usage data to understand how the app is used, identify common issues, and improve the user experience. This data does not contain any information that could identify you personally. Examples include crash reports, feature usage statistics, and general device information (e.g., OS version). This data is collected in a way that is compliant with Apple's App Store Review Guidelines.
  • Technical Data: In the event of an app crash or error, we may receive anonymized technical data (e.g., device model, OS version, time of crash). This data is used solely for diagnostic purposes to improve the app's stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used exclusively to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Payday App and its features.
  • Understand and analyze how you use the App and what products and services are most relevant to you.
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues and improve stability.
  • Develop new features and functionality.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

  • No Selling of Data: Payday will NEVER sell your personal or financial data to third parties. Our business model is not based on monetizing your information.
  • No Third-Party Access to Financial Data: Your financial data remains on your device and is not shared with any third-party services or individuals.
  • Aggregated/Anonymized Data: We may share aggregated or anonymized usage data with partners or for research purposes, but this data cannot be used to identify you personally.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with legal obligations.

4. Data Security

We are committed to protecting the security of your information. While your financial data is stored locally on your device, we implement robust security measures for any anonymized usage data that may be transmitted, consistent with industry best practices. Please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices and Rights

Since we do not collect personally identifiable information, there are no specific accounts or profiles for you to manage. You are in control of the data on your device. You can typically delete the app from your device to remove all locally stored data.

6. Children's Privacy

Payday is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information from our systems.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
