Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

"We believe your financial data is yours alone. We built Payday to help you, not to harvest your information."

Our Commitment

Payday ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Financial Data: Transaction history, account balances, and spending patterns (encrypted and stored locally where possible)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version for troubleshooting
  • Usage Data: Anonymous app usage statistics to improve features

How We Protect Your Data

We implement bank-level security measures:

  • 256-bit AES encryption for all data transmission and storage
  • Biometric authentication support (Face ID/Touch ID)
  • We cannot see your bank passwords or login credentials
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Data anonymization for analytics

What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties
  • We do not share your financial information with advertisers
  • We do not track your location beyond what is necessary for fraud prevention
  • We do not store your bank login credentials on our servers

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and download your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications
  • Update or correct your information

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

privacy@payday.app

By using Payday, you agree to this Privacy Policy. We may update this policy from time to time, and will notify you of significant changes via the app or email.
